Namibia Marine Products

Making the right deals. Catching the right opportunities.

A family-run Namibian fishing business built on trust, structure, and real partnerships.

Our Approach

NMP Marine Products is a Namibian fishing company that brokers and arranges fishing deals with other companies and operators. We work across all species and deal types — if there's a fishing opportunity that makes commercial sense, we pursue it. NMP also holds two fishing licenses that we lease out to vessel operators, allowing other boats to fish on our licenses under structured agreements.

How We Work

Fishing Deal Brokering

We arrange fishing deals with companies and operators across Namibia and beyond. No matter the species or scale, if it's a deal worth making, we make it happen.

License Leasing

NMP holds two active fishing licenses that we lease out to vessel operators. This allows other boats to fish commercially under our licenses, under clear and structured leasing terms.

Flexible Deal Structures

We don't limit ourselves to a single fish type or deal format. Whether it's a short-term arrangement or a long-standing partnership, we structure deals to suit the opportunity and the people involved.
